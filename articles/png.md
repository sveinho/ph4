PNG was created as a patent-free alternative to GIF, optimizing web graphics, screenshots, and digital illustrations.

#### 1. Recommendation Criteria
Highly recommended for digital-only images, web graphics, and charts requiring alpha transparency or sharp text lines without compression artifacts.

#### 2. Migration Risks
Very stable for general digital ecosystems. However, for extreme long-term preservation of historical cultural heritage, master files should ideally be stored as uncompressed TIFF.

#### 3. Technical Limitations
Does not natively support CMYK color spaces, making it unsuited and debated for professional print workflows. File sizes are also much larger than lossy JPEGs for photographs.

### Format Identification
- **Byte Signature (Magic Bytes):** Starts with ` PNG\r\n\u001a\n` (Hex: `89 50 4E 47 0D 0A 1A 0A`).
- **Structural Analysis:** Structured around chunks (IHDR, IDAT, IEND) providing data metadata, pixel content, and termination markers.
- **Content Analysis:** PRONOM PUID `fmt/11`.
